2048+ support Magic Trackpad, you can use two fingers swipe to move the tiles.

Magic Mouse is also supported, use one finger swipe to move the tiles.

Further more, you can use your arrow keys or [WSDA] keys to move the tiles.

When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one!

When the board fills up, game over.
